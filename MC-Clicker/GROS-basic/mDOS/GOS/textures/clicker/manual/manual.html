Mines
Description:
Mine is foundation of this game. Allow you collect resources for sell, crafting and furnace. In game exist two kinds of mines listed below.

Stone mine

Description:
This is standard mine where you can found undeground materials. It have infinity depth, and is splited to five different biomes:
Stone mine
Deepslate mine
Pitstone mine
Netherrack mine
The end
Evrytime when you mine block below you new generated block can be set to random material dependent on depth.

Crafting levels

Description:
In crafting you can craft in-game items from inventory resources. To craft better item you must construct upgrades to crafting.

Basic crafting table

Can craft:
Any sticks, Any planks, gold nuggets, gold ingots, stone crafting upgrade

Can craft:
Any sticks, Any planks, gold nuggets, gold ingots, iron crafting upgrade, stone tools

Furnace levels

Description:
In Furnace you can smet raw ores to ingots. If you will smelt any material furnace must be fueled first. Fuel list can be found below.

Basic furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron
Smelt time: 8000ms

Iron furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold
Smelt time: 6000ms

Gold furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold
Smelt time: 4000ms

Diamond furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold
Smelt time: 2000ms

Emerald furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold
Smelt time: 1000ms

Ruby furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold, Ancient debris
Smelt time: 100ms

Netherite furnace

Can smelt:
Raw copper, Raw iron, Raw gold, Ancient debris
Smelt time: INSTANT
